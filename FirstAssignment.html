I am a 3rd year Medical Sciences student at Brock, and this is list of the courses I am taking throughout this school year:

  1. First Semester
    • Web Media Production (IASC 1P02)
    • Nutrition in Health and Disease (HLSC 3P93)
    • Cardiac Pathophysiology (HLSC 3P90)
    • The Health of Canadian Society (HLSC 2P21)
  2. Second Semester
    • Medical Microbiology (HLSC 3P97)
    • Principles of Pharmacology (HLSC 3P19)
    • Bioethics (BIOL 2P95)
    • Processes of Health and Wellness (HLSC 3P92)
